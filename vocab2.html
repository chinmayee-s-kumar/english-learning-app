🗂️ Vocabulary: Synonyms & Antonyms

Lesson 2: Synonyms and Antonyms

1️⃣ Synonyms

Synonyms are words that have similar meanings.

Happy – Joyful, Cheerful, Glad
Example: She felt happy → She felt joyful after winning.
Big – Large, Huge, Enormous
Example: The house is big → The house is huge.
Smart – Intelligent, Clever, Bright
Example: He is very smart → He is very clever.
💡 Tip: Learn synonyms in groups of 5–10 words and use them in sentences.

2️⃣ Antonyms

Antonyms are words that have opposite meanings.

Hot – Cold
Example: The tea is hot → The tea is cold.
Happy – Sad
Example: She is happy → She is sad.
Fast – Slow
Example: The car is fast → The car is slow.
💡 Tip: Remember antonyms as pairs and practice them daily to expand your vocabulary.

3️⃣ Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Write synonyms for the following words:

  • Small → _____
  • Angry → _____
  • Beautiful → _____

Exercise 2: Write antonyms for the following words:

  • Hot → _____
  • Big → _____
  • Happy → _____

Exercise 3: Make 2 sentences using synonyms or antonyms of “Smart”.

Answers Exercise 1:

  • Small → Tiny, Little
  • Angry → Furious, Mad
  • Beautiful → Pretty, Lovely

Answers Exercise 2:

  • Hot → Cold
  • Big → Small
