💬 Conversation Practice: Talking About Hobbies

Lesson 2: Talking About Hobbies

Talking about hobbies is a great way to make conversations interesting. Learn how to ask and answer about hobbies.

1️⃣ Common Phrases to Talk About Hobbies

  • What do you like to do in your free time?
  • My hobby is [hobby].
  • I enjoy [activity].
  • Do you like [activity]?
  • I love [hobby/activity].
💡 Tip: Use phrases like “I enjoy…” or “I love…” to sound more natural.

2️⃣ Example Dialogues

Dialogue 1: Asking About Hobbies
A: Hi Riya! What do you like to do in your free time?
B: Hi! I enjoy reading books and painting. How about you?
A: I love playing football and watching movies.
Dialogue 2: Sharing Hobbies
A: Hello! Do you like music?
B: Yes, I love listening to music and singing.
A: That’s great! I enjoy playing guitar in my free time.
Dialogue 3: Hobby Discussion
A: What is your favorite hobby?
B: My favorite hobby is gardening.
A: That’s interesting! I enjoy cooking and trying new recipes.

3️⃣ Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Write 3 sentences about your hobbies using “I enjoy” or “I love”.

Exercise 2: Make a short dialogue with a friend asking about hobbies.

Exercise 3: Identify hobby phrases in this sentence:

"I enjoy swimming and I love painting. What about you?"

Answers Exercise 3:

  • Hobby phrases: I enjoy swimming, I love painting
  • Question phrase: What about you?
💡 Tip: Speak about hobbies you genuinely like. It makes conversations more engaging.
